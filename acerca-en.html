About Psyletics

Discover our history and mission


Who are we?

Psyletics is an innovative platform that combines sports psychology with modern technology. Our goal is to help athletes reach their full mental and emotional potential.


Our Mission

We provide specialized psychological support for athletes of all levels, from beginners to professionals, using advanced digital tools and personalized care.


Why Psyletics?

We believe mental health is just as important as physical health in sports. Our team of specialized psychologists understands the unique challenges athletes face and offers effective solutions.


Our Services

  • • Chat with specialized Artificial Intelligence
  • • Personalized daily emotional tracking
  • • Consultations with sports psychologists
  • • Meditation and relaxation tools
  • • Mental training programs

Our Team

We have a multidisciplinary team of sports psychologists, developers, and mental wellness specialists, all committed to your athletic and personal success.

We offer specialized psychological support for athletes who want to reach their highest mental performance.
